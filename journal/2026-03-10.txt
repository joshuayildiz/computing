is the least bad thing about being unemployed.

The last couple years we (the whole world) saw the purchasing power
of already retired people decline rapidly. They cannot buy anything
but groceries with their salaries and the little they can get is
less than satisfactory, subpar quality food stuff. They are hungering
away to the system that they once slaved away for years in hopes

So even if I had a job right now, my efforts would give no fruit

retirment paychecks.

Not only that, but for the current retirees even selling their own
